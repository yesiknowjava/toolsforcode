Are you looking to buy a new domain. Chances are that you might of heard of godaddy, bluehost, namecheap and many more. Indeed there are a lot of service providers in this domain and you might be actually overwhelmed with choices.

An important consideration for many people is whether the domain registrar also provide for hosting. That way there is no need to confirm domain ownership. It also saves time because, the name servers ns1 to ns4 are already the right ones to use and you need not go through the hastle of adding the name servers of hosting providers into the name servers of the domain registrar.

Also, many registrars provide attractive first year discounts. They also sometimes provide great discounts for transferring a domain from another registrar to theirs. The following is a good list of quite popular domain registrars.
